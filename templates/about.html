About

Welcome to DocuQuiz! I built this tool to help you turn any PDF into a set of multiple-choice questions — perfect for studying, revision, or testing yourself on the go.

How to use

  1. Head over to Upload PDF and pick a PDF file from your device.
  2. Choose how many questions you want (1–30).
  3. Pick a difficulty level — Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  4. Hit Generate MCQs and let the AI do the rest.
  5. Go through the quiz and click an answer to see if you got it right.

Tips

  • Text-based PDFs work best. If your PDF is scanned or just images, the text might not come through.
  • Hard mode works better with content that has some depth to it.
  • You can always revisit your last quiz under the Generated Questions tab.
